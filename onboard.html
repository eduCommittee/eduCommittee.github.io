Mission Statement

Our mission statement is divided into three sections: what we do, our target audience, and what makes us different. It defines the vision and scope of the projects that we will work on.

What We Do

We focus on pedagogical problems that traditional education systems often leave unsolved, using mathematical modeling, algorithms, and system design.

  • Pedagogical Game Theory

    Developing educational games using finite automata

  • Algorithmic Pedagogy

    Designing effective and scalable learning algorithms

  • Course Design

    Creating formal and recreational educational courses

  • Software Solutions

    Designing learning systems and pedagogical tools

Target Audience

Our programs are designed for autodidacts, educators, and classroom students who want to optimize their learning experience.

  • Autodidacts

    Individuals seeking effective and autonomous learning experiences

  • Educators

    Teachers and trainers who want to improve educational outcomes based on empirical pedagogical research

  • Students

    Students struggling with mathematical learning due to accumulated learning gaps

  • Researchers

    Contributors aiming to test, measure, and improve learning methodologies

What Makes Us Different

We're not reinventing the wheel, but our value proposition is unprecedented in our home country. Most of what makes us different is detailed in our founding document.

  • Pedagogy-based

    We're a pedagogically-grounded team working to conceive of solutions validated by empirical pedagogical research.

  • Values

    We're equipped with a contrarian philosophy for what education and learning is that helps us enmesh the right problems to work on.

  • Exploratory Problem Modeling

    We take enough time to properly diagnose educational problems from first principles, not with generic anecdotes.

  • Intrinsic Motivation

    We genuinely want to solve pedagogical deficiency. We do it because we love it.

Anyone interested in this problem space is welcome. You don't have to be an expert in anything.
